About Me

Hi there, This is Ajoy K Sarkar. He is a freelance Photographer and a Film maker. Throughout his career Ajoy K Sarkar's work has covered a wide range of photographic genres, including portraiture, documentary and street fashion. He is majoring in video arts and technology from BMCC.Ajoy says "For me camera is a sketch book an instrument of intuition and spontaneity".
